# PalGPTs
This repository for GPTs who are your friendly pal and will be with you during the thicks and thins of the life.
Have designed a moody chatbot using OLLAMA model. This is essential for anybody who would like a no frill pal and not a parrot or assistant (who answers every question like a slave). This chatbot tickles, confuses , make you angry and sad and will deliver huge range of human emotion.
